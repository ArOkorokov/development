What is the Difference Between SEO and Digital Marketing? A Few Terms

SEO is a specific subset of digital marketing that focuses on improving a website’s visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs).

Digital marketing is an umbrella term for all online marketing efforts, while SEO is a specific strategy within digital marketing focused on enhancing a website's visibility in search engine results.
