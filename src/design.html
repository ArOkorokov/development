Bento UI

Most Popular Trend in UI/UX Design 2024

Bento UI is a web design style that uses colorful cards or tiles to create a visually engaging layout. These tiles can be arranged in a uniform or varied pattern and often feature bright colors and images to draw attention

Inspired by Japanese 'bento' boxes, which neatly organize different foods into separate compartments, this design approach is versatile, appealing to all age groups, and easily combines with other web design styles

Though neo brutalism isn’t ideal for every website, it can give a site a distinctive and memorable personality, helping it stand out from the competition
